About the collection

Here is a small collection of quotes from various more or less famous authors in diﬀerent languages (with English translations where needed). All quotes are divided into folders that correspond to the languages in which they were originally written. Here and there additional subfolders were made when necessary.

This ﬁle was specially compiled by Michael Demidov to illustrate the work of the script for assembling a tree-like table of contents for HTML, see the GitHub. (And, by God, collecting them took almost more time than writing the script itself!)

Ay fu o charity.

Note About Old Russian Language

This is an old Russian language that was in use until the beginning of the twentieth century. Of course, it is more similar to modern Russian than the language of medieval English ballads is similar to modern English. The main diﬀerence is the use of old Cyrillic letters “ѣ”/“ѣ” (yat) and “i”.
